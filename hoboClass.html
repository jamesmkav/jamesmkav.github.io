Hobo Actions

No Mailing Address - @LEVEL 1 = No Speeding Tickets.

Loitering - @LEVEL 1 = Just do nothing. After rolling, you may choose to do nothing instead of moving. In this case, you stay on the space and nothing happens. The next turn proceeds as normal.

Not in my back yard - @LEVEL 1 = If you land on a property space on your roll, double your Mobility that turn by rolling a second die and adding it to your movement. If there is a Police character currently playing, they jump to the property space.

Panhandling - @LEVEL 1 = Every player passed, take 10% of their total money, rounded to the two highest digets. If they are in debt, then nothing happens.

Pass the buck - @LEVEL 4 = Whatever current space you land on after rolling, any negative effects as a result, including any Jail Time are passed on to any other player in the same Section of the board of your choosing. This includes the Life of Crime and The Bank Robbery sections. If there are no other players in the same section, the negative effects normally affect the Hobo character.

Hobo Perks

Hard Living - @LEVEL 1, +5K XP. every turn.

Non Profit Program - @LEVEL 2 = +10K XP. every turn.

Insurance settlements - @LEVEL 2 = ( +$10K * Mobility roll) every turn.

Rock Bottom - @LEVEL 4 = +100K XP

Hobo Weaknesses

- Your Mobility is 1 die through the whole game. This includes any actions that require a Mobility roll.

- You cannot own property.

- @LEVEL 4 = Each turn, you must do a 10 second confession of something you regret in your life. You have 30 seconds to think of something. Have fun.
