CONGRATULATIONS CRIMINAL!

You've successfully defeated your opponents and won!

There's nothing that can prepare you for a successful life of crime. You just make it happen.

Once your competitors had been disposed of, you live a long and happy life in a Columbian mansion. Spending your days executing traitors to your cause and deciding what superpowers to help, only to further your influence throughout the third world. The dark web trembles at the mention of your name. By the time you are of old age, you don't even need to break out of prison any more. They just show you the way. When you die, everyone will suspect you faked it, even though you didn't and your legacy will live on for decades.
