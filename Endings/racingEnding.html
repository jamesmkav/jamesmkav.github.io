CONGRATULATIONS RACER!

You've successfully defeated your opponents and won!

Speed is a drug, are you are addicted. You've beat everyone on Earth in every discipline of serious racing and it's still not enough. Through a one night stand, you have a daughter. As a result, you find your purpose in life. She grows up under your wing and becomes a formidable racing opponent and eventually is the first female F1 driver. She wins every race in the season and dedicates every one of them to you beforehand. When you die, the do a lap with the hearse at Monaco before the race. Your grave is adorned with racing paraphenalia and for the next several decades, the cemetary attendant complains that he shouldn't have to move so many tires.
