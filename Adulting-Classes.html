The police officer has a number of advantages over the other players in terms of movement. This allows for a predatory strategy where you look for clusters of other players to pass over and charge for speeding tickets. The cop is the obvious foil to the criminal character, or anyone that is obviously going to be attempting to go for "The Bank Robbery". You can travel freely through the "Life of Crime" but you cannot be affected by any of the consequences, good or bad. You also have your own special space in the middle of the board, allowing you to wait for other players slightly behind you with bad intentions.

As the Politician, you are at the mercy of the people. You'll rise and fall in the policial world but don't let that stop you from having a family or being as corrupt as your respective party! You can still recieve promotions the hard way but it doesn't hurt to have a few elections swing in your favor.

Zoom zoom! As the Racing career, you will experience high speeds and living dangerously. You can move quickly, and if necessary, squeeze out an extra space in a pinch. When you are out in the woods, you can move double!

The Spy character is slippery and uses his opponents strengths against them. Like a chameleon, the spy has an arsenal of disguises that they can emply for any situation. They do answer to someone though and aren't deadly until later in their career. Until then you must use the attributes of the other players to your advantage.

DEGREE REQUIREDThe assassin can decide to attempt a hit before or after moving their game piece but not both in one turn. They can attempt a hit on as many players as are within valid distance during that turn, strating with any payer that is within 3 spaces, not including your present space.

After rolling for movement, you may choose to do nothing instead of moving after you see the zone card or effects of the space. In this instance, you move back to your space before movement, plus 2 spaces and discard the zone card.

DEGREE REQUIREDEach turn, you can try to invent something. This is done by rolling 4 dice. If you get a 20 or higher, draw an item card. You may use it at any point during your current or subsequent turn.

At any point during your turn, you may place a token on the space you are occupying. This token can be teleported to before rolling for movement during any subsequent turn and is picked back up when you do so.

DEGREE REQUIREDAny player passed by you goes to jail for 1 Turn. You may also use the Speed Trap when passing through any of the START spaces. You may choose to park here and continue movement on your next roll. You may stay parked as many turns as you like. You receive no benefits or consequences in “Life of Crime”.

DEGREE REQUIREDEach turn, before moving, a vote is cast. The politician picks either even or odd, and a specific number. If the majority of players roll a number that corresponds to the politician's guess, they recieve a promotion. If not or there is a tie, they are demoted. If the politician guess the total of all players rolls with their specific number, they recieve $2,000,000 instantly. All other Promotions and Demotions through normal play apply as well.

You may use 3 dice for movement

DEGREE REQUIREDEvery player you pass, you can try to sell something to. This is done by both players rolling two dice. If you roll a higher total, take the total of your dice and multiply it x10K and recieve this amount of money from the player.

Take on the perks of any player in the same zone as you. You can only use one player's perks per turn.
