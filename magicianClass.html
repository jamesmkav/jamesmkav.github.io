Magician

When you are a master of illusion, nothing can keep you pinned. You can slip in and out of jail with the greatest of ease. Vegas will bend to your tricks and even assassins will have a hard time defeating you. Once you start climbing your career ladder, you become more mobile and able to jump great distances quickly on the board. Though you and your family will never settle down, you will have a rich life with lots of stories on both sides of the law.


Magicican Strengths

@LEVEL 2= On any turn, you may jump to the Start space. This counts as your turn.
@LEVEL 2= Any player passed, the magician can try to perofrm a trick. This is done by trying to roll a yahtzee in 4 rolls. If the yahtzee is successful, the player pays the magician whatever the magician's current salary is.
@LEVEL 2= Double all winnings in "Let it Ride".
@LEVEL 3= Upon any arrest and jail sentence, you escape on the next turn. Go to the Start Space and proceed as normal.
@LEVEL 3= If the assassin attempts a hit and succeeds, you may try to counter by rolling a number higher than what the assassin rolled.
@LEVEL 3= +1 Mobility
@LEVEL 4= +$1,000,000 each turn while in "Let it Ride".
@LEVEL 5= +$2,000,000 each turn while in "Let it Ride". (In addition to the Lvl. 4 $1M bonus.)

Magicican Weaknesses

The Magician character cannot have any property.
