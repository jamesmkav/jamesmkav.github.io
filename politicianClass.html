Politician

As the Politician, you are at the mercy of the people. You'll rise and fall in the policial world but don't let that stop you from having a family or being as corrupt as your respective party! You can still recieve promotions the hard way but it doesn't hurt to have a few elections swing in your favor.


Politician Strengths

@LEVEL 4= You may offer a full pardon to any player in Jail during your turn. They pay you $1M to be released on their next turn.
@LEVEL 4= You recieve $1M for every player in Jail during your turn. This can include any pardons paid for.
@LEVEL 6= You cannot go to jail. Anytime you go to Jail, you just go immediately to Start.



Politician Weaknesses

@LEVEL 1= Every time you are in for a Promotion, an election is held to decide if you recieve it. The players each roll 4 dice. Any player that gets 13 or higher counts as one vote. If you win the majority of player's votes, you recieve a promotion. If you lose the election, you recieve nothing. If there is an even amount of players and there is a tie, the politician rolls a tiebreaker vote. This is ina ddition to any Life or Near Death Experiences necessary for the promotion.
You only recieve half of any money from a successful bank robbery.
For any promotion to Lvl. 4, you must be Married with at least one Child.
