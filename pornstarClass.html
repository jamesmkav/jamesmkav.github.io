Pornstar

The pornstar has a hard road ahead of them. Theres some stiff competition. But if they just put their head down and thrust forward, theres a shaft of light ahead. You can use your looks and sesuality to get out of tickets. No one will take you seriously, until they worship you. The pornstar character recieves more financial gain with greater numbers of players in a game. If you keep pulling favors along the way, victory can be yours.


Pornstar Strengths

@LEVEL 2= For any player passed, you may perform a Favor for them. Roll 2 dice to see what Favor they would like and the effect.
@LEVEL 2= Pay half on all speeding tickets.
@LEVEL 2= +$1M for every year spent in jail.
@LEVEL 3= +1 Child
@LEVEL 4= +2 Child
@LEVEL 4= For each player in the game, recieve $100K salary bonus each turn.
@LEVEL 6= For each player in the game, recieve $300K salary bonus each turn.

Pornstar Weaknesses

The Pornstar cannot get married.
