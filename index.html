I worked my way up each ladder in front of me with technique, tenacity and professionalism while learning a lifetime worth of lessons.

I pondered what was next and eventually came back around to technology. I began learning the fundamentals of web design and programming on my own and decided to further my knowledge by attending The Tech Academy in PDX. It was through this that I began to understand the potential behind the basics I had learned.

 I use my tenacity and technique from being a Chef to learn new languages and tools to progress in my projects. I use my hospitality minded outset to build a seamless and streamlined user experience, as I believe, that 'user' is another name for a 'customer'. Which as a restaurant proprietor, you want plenty of!

I absolutely love to build new things in development. It provides and immense sense of accomplishment and it is always fantastic to see someone use and enjoy the thing you just made. Just like in cooking, I am forever a student. I like to tackle large projects in team environments so I can learn and sometimes teach tricks of the trade. When I'm not developing software, I like to cook, play music, practice martial arts and enjoy the outdoors of the Pacific Northwest with my amazing wife and cat.
