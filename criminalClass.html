Criminal

The Life of Crime is not for everyone, but those that decide to walk it's path will find a dangerous path to possible riches. You will be in and out of jail but this will ultimately help you. If you can live long enough to be the boss, you'll be handing out death like you're dealing cards. You may go into debt at the beginning, but it won't be a problem. With your rep, you'll rule the streets in no time. Soo woo mothafucka!



Criminal Strengths

Low friends in high places - @LEVEL 1, All degrees are able to be purchased at $100K each.

Street Cred - @LEVEL 1, +1 Promotion for every Jail sentence.

Local Reputation - @LEVEL 2, +100K XP for any Mobility total less than half of the current highest possible Mobility roll

Pickpocketing - @LEVEL 3, Any player passed, you can attempt a pickpocket on. This is done by the two players rolling 4 dice each. If the Criminal rolls higher, the pickpocket is successful. The reward for a successful pickpocket is 10% of the other players $. If they are in debt, there is no effect and play resumes

Local Lottery - @LEVEL 4, Each turn, every player in the same section as the Criminal rolls 1 dice. Whoever rolls the highest, recives $1M. If there are no other players in the same section, the $1M autmatically goes to the Criminal. If the Police character is in the same section, the Criminal serves Jail Time of 1 Turn and recieves no one recieves lottery winnings.

Family War promotion - @LEVEL 5, +$7M

Kill your Competitors - @LEVEL 6, Every other character in the same section and each directly joining section has a Near Death Experience, including the Criminal, upon promotion.

Going Legit - @LEVEL 6, Recive half of winnings for any game won on The Strip by any other player. As a result, they recive half of their winnings as well.

Criminal Weaknesses

- +1 Turn to any Jail sentence
- No family after Lvl. 3
- For promotion to Lvl. 5, someone has to die. After you pass the promotion space and qualify, the next player you pass on the board has a Near Death Experience and you recieve the promotion.
- @LEVEL 5, Each turn, before rolling for Mobility, have a Near Death Experience.
- @LEVEL 6, Each turn, give up an attribute permenently.
